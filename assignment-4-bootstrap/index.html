The Starbucks College Achievement Plan makes a college affordable!

We know the cost of college can be overwhelming. That's why the Starbucks College Achievement Plan provides benefits-eligible partners with 100% tuition coverage at Arizona State University

How will I fund college?

  • Each semester you'll receive a 42% scholarship toward undergraduate tuition at ASU
  • You're responsible for paying the remaining out-of-pocket tuition after the scholarship is applied at the beginning of the semester. The Starbucks College Achievement Plan doesn't cover books or special course fees.
  • Near the end of the semester, Starbucks will reimburse any tuition costs not covered by the need-based financial aid directly in your paycheck.
